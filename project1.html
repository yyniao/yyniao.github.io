    A robot always says hello to you

This is a robot that can say hello to you. Whenever you walk into it or say hello to it, the robot will wave to you affectionately. A good morning greeting, a greeting after lunch, bring you a happy day.

Use Arduino to make it possible.

Arduino can sense the environment through a variety of sensors, and feedback and influence the environment by controlling lights, motors and other devices. The microcontroller on the board can be programmed by Arduino programming language, compiled into binary files, and burned into the microcontroller. Arduino is programmed by Arduino programming language (based on wiring) and Arduino development environment (based on processing). The project based on Arduino can only contain Arduino, or it can also include Arduino and other software running on PC. they communicate with each other (such as flash, processing, maxmsp).
