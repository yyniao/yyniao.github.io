Our Goal

Our final project mainly wants to be an arithmetic tool for early childhood education. Nowadays, many parents in the first and second grades will be asked by teachers to give their children arithmetic problems at home. This is a waste of time in itself, but for the children’s study, parents have to write the questions one by one, and finally pay Need to mark the answers one by one for the children. Therefore, now there is an app program that specializes in arithmetic problems, and he can randomly generate arithmetic problems for children to do. But the problem has arisen again. Often this kind of applications based on mobile phones and tablets, the push of other APPs in the device will interfere with children's problems, and it is very likely that children will be attracted by the games on the tablet when the parents are not paying attention. While playing games, you cannot concentrate on completing tasks. Therefore, we hope to use arduino to make a "question machine", which can automatically randomly display arithmetic questions of addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division on the screen. By connecting the input device, the child enters the answer according to the question displayed on the screen in the input device. If the answer is correct, the machine will play the correct sound, if the answer is wrong, the machine will play the wrong sound. The advanced program can be set to a competition mode, such as a limited time. If the requirements (accuracy rate or number of questions) are not met within the limited time, it means the game has failed. In summary, we have made a "question machine" in order to save parents' time, and secondly to prevent children from playing with mobile phones and tablets, and the question machine itself can stimulate children's desire to win and lose like a game.
